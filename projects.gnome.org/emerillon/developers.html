Emerillon is free software available under the GNU General Public License. You can help improving it in several ways.

Contributing

You can get the source code to Emerillon from the download page.

Translation

Emerillon is ready to be translated. Follow the instructions on GNOME's wiki and give a hand on GNOME's translation management system for the Emerillon module.

Dependencies

Emerillon and its shipped plugins depend on:

  • Gtk+
  • libchamplain 0.4
  • librest
  • Clutter 1.0
  • Geoclue
  • Ethos

Bug reporting

Bugs can be reported on GNOME's bugtracker under the Emerillon product.

Get in touch with the team

Emerillon developers hang out on #champlain on the Freenode IRC network.
