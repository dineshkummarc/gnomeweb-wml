snappy

snappy is an open source media player that gathers the power and flexibility of gstreamer inside the comfort of a minimalistic clutter interface.

Requirements

  • glib 2.10
  • GStreamer 0.10
  • clutter 1.6
  • clutter-gst 1.4
  • Optional: gio 2.26

Bugs

If you want to report bugs please use the GNOME Bug Tracking System. by reporting your bug here.
Please try to test your bug against the development version of snappy first. Your problem may already be fixed!
