Gnome Scan should be installable through your distribution. Don't hesitate to ask for Gnome Scan in your distribution!

Feedbacks

Bugs must be reported to the Gnome Bug Tracking System.

Please send debug output when you report a new bug in Gnome Scan.

Don't hesitate to test latest SVN of Gnome Scan and improve it. Patches welcome !
