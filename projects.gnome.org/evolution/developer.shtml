IRC Team Meetings

The Evolution team gets together on Wednesdays at 15:30 UTC for a status meeting on IRC. Anyone is welcome to attend. The meetings are held in #evolution-meet on irc.gimp.org, and are usually about a half-hour long.

Update: As of May 2010, the team meetings are now monthly instead of weekly. They are held on the third Wednesday of every month.
