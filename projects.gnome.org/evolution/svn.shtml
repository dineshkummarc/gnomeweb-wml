SVN Access

The modules and branches required for building Evolution, from newest version to oldest are listed below. In you need general help read the GNOME SVN FAQ.

All of the following modules are available from GNOME SVN.

Development (2.25.x)

Module Branch
evolution trunk
evolution-data-server trunk
gtkhtml trunk
libsoup trunk
evolution-exchange trunk(optional)
