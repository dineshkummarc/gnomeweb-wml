Gnumeric 1.10.6 aka "TBD" is now available.

The Gnumeric Team is pleased to announce the availability of Gnumeric version 1.10.6. For best results, use with the concurrently released goffice 0.8.6.

Noteworthy news in this release:

  • Tooltips assist with entering function argument.
  • The Statistics menu was moved to the top-level menubar.
  • The yyyy-mm-dd format will be present in all locales.
