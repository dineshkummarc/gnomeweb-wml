Gnumeric 1.10.2 aka "White Zebra" is now available.

The Gnumeric Team is pleased to announce the availability of Gnumeric version 1.10.2. This release require the concurrently release goffice 0.8.2. The recently released libgsf 1.14.18 is recommended as it fixes an ODF export problem.

Noteworthy news in this release:

  • win32 fixes.
  • ODF export fixes.
  • XLS export fixes for cell comments and calls to XIRR.
