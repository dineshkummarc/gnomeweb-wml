Gnumeric 1.10.9 aka "TBD" is now available.

The Gnumeric Team is pleased to announce the availability of Gnumeric version 1.10.9. The concurrently released Goffice 0.8.9 is recommended.

Noteworthy news in this release:

  • Improved chart export/import for ODF.
  • Improved column/row resizing.
  • New test infrastructure to intentionally corrupt the contents of xml and xml-inside-zip while leaving syntactically correct files.
  • Improved handling of corrupted Gnumeric and ODF files.
  • An error in evaluation of certain circular formulas was fixed.
