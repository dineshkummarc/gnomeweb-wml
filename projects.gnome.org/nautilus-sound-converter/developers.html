Getting Involved

To get involved, contact maintainer Nautilus-Sound-ConverterMaintainer

Just as many other GNOME applications, Nautilus-Sound-Converter is translated by the GNOME translation team.


Downloads

Nautilus-Sound-Converter should be available with your distribution.

The latest releases can always be found at the Google Code Page.
