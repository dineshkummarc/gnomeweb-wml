Looking to GNOME 2.8 and Beyond

GNOME operates on a time-based release philosophy, an attempt to continuously provide the best of our developers' efforts to users as quickly as we can. Six months after GNOME 2.6, we anticipate that GNOME 2.8 will feature integrated email, contacts, calendar, and multimedia functionality, and further advances in accessibility, usability and internationalization.
