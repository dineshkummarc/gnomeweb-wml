Copyright Information

In exchange for your grant of copyright, Ximian does two things. First, we promise to use the GPL or another Debian-endorsed Free license for your code (although we may also grant other licenses on other terms). Second, we grant back to you as author all your original rights to do what you want with your code, without any fees or restrictions from us. This includes your right to relicense under whatever terms you may desire.

What Requires Assignment

Very subtle or very difficult bug fixes of any number of lines will always require copyright assignment. However, the following types of changes do not require assignment:

Your grant lets Ximian use your code, but you keep your original ownership rights as well.
  • Most changes under five lines.
  • Translations.
  • Typos and other "trivial" changes.
  • Documentation.
  • Artwork.
  • A collection of simple one-line changes, even if the total number of lines changes is greater than five.

How to Assign Copyright
