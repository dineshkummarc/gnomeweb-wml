Overview

Evolution provides integrated mail, addressbook and calendaring functionality to users of the GNOME desktop.

Keep track of the latest developments at the Evolution project wiki, participate in the Evolution community with mailing lists, and chat with the developers and users of Evolution using an IRC client.

What's currently happening with Evolution?

Keep up-to-date by tracking the most recent releases and development news at the Evolution project wiki.

Internet Relay Chat

Discussion of Evolution (development and otherwise) takes place in #evolution on irc.gimpnet.org.

Also on IRC — every Thursday, from 1500 GMT to 0100 GMT — are Evolution Bug Days. Help squash bugs with the development team!

Mailing Lists

Since there's a lot to talk about when it comes to Evolution, we have split the discussions into three different mailing lists.
  • General Questions
  • Hacking Questions
  • Patch Review
