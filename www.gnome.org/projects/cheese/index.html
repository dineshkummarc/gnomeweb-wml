What is Cheese?

Cheese uses your webcam to take photos and videos, applies fancy special effects and lets you share the fun with others. It was written as part of Google's 2007 Summer of Code lead by daniel g. siegel and mentored by RaphaÃ«l Slinckx, and has most of the classical photo booth features after a bare couple of months of development. Under the hood, Cheese uses GStreamer to apply fancy effects to photos and videos. After a success of the Summer of Code, the development continued and we still are looking for people with nice ideas and patches ;)

What are the Requirements?

  • GNOME 2.22
  • GStreamer 0.10
  • postr for Flickr export (optional)
  • f-spot for F-Spot export (optional)
  • a webcam
  • a brain

For a more detailed list of requirements, have a look at the FAQ

Bugs

Well, if you want to keep them, they are yours, we donate them to you! But if you want to bug some people and kick some ass, you should report them to the GNOME Bug Tracking System.
Also, please try to test your bug against the SVN version of Cheese first. Your problem may already be fixed!

News

March 11th 2008

Check out the brand new Cheese Tour! We tried to cover all features of Cheese and present them to you in a nice way, such that you get a quick overview of Cheese.

March 10th 2008

Stable verson 2.22.0 was released! New features since previous stable version include:

  • brand new help/documentation pages
  • drop libglade and use GtkBuilder instead.
  • replaced all gnome-vfs calls by gio
  • use a bugreport script, which automatically attaches needed information to a bug report
  • brand new effect images
  • Plug some memory leaks
  • Run webcam detection in background thread and show a nice throbber
  • a gigantic number of bug fixes
  • added/updated translations
Download it (full release notes)

January 14th 2008

Unstable verson 2.21.5 was released! New features since previous stable version include:

  • version bump to follow the gnome release cycle
  • add a stylish countdown widget
  • use the gst-player by Mathias Hasselman for playing the shutter sound
  • make cheese build against uninstalled gstreamer
  • a lot of bug fixes
  • added/updated translations
Download it (full release notes)
