What is Cheese?

Cheese uses your webcam to take photos and videos, applies fancy special effects and lets you share the fun with others. It was written as part of Google's 2007 Summer of Code lead by daniel g. siegel and mentored by RaphaÃ«l Slinckx Under the hood, Cheese uses GStreamer to apply fancy effects to photos and videos. With Cheese it is easy to take photos of you, your friends, pets or whatever you want and share them with others. After a success of the Summer of Code, the development continued and we still are looking for people with nice ideas and patches ;)

What are the Requirements?

  • GNOME 2.22
  • GStreamer 0.10
  • postr for Flickr export (optional)
  • f-spot for F-Spot export (optional)
  • a webcam
  • a brain

For a more detailed list of requirements, have a look at the FAQ

Bugs

Well, if you want to keep them, they are yours, we donate them to you! But if you want to bug some people and kick some ass, you should report them to the GNOME Bug Tracking System.
Also, please try to test your bug against the SVN version of Cheese first. Your problem may already be fixed!

News

August 5th 2008

Unstable verson 2.23.6 was released! New features since previous version include:

  • add a fullscreen mode for kiosks
  • display a loading icon while generating thumbnail and a fallback icon if thumbnail fails
  • resolve sluggish video and out-of-sync audio
  • added/updated translations
Download it (full release notes)

July 21st 2008

Unstable verson 2.23.5 was released! New features since previous version include:

  • make recording of videos work again
  • write logs, when cheese crashes
  • allow to choose the webcam using the preference dialog
  • start cheese when you plug in your camera
  • use ogv as default video extension
  • added/updated translations
Download it (full release notes)

June 29th 2008

Stable verson 2.22.3 was released! New features since previous stable version include:

  • cheese finally selects the right thumbnails for already taken photos
  • added/updated translations
Download it (full release notes)

June 16th 2008

Unstable verson 2.23.4 was released! New features since previous version include:

  • fix a crash, when using about/quit menu items in hildon mode
  • Instead of crashing report when certain gstreamer are not found
  • use intltools 0.40 for building with autotools
  • added/updated translations
