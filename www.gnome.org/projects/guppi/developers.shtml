Guppi Developers

The original version of Guppi was primarily the work of Havoc Pennington. Anything cool about Guppi probably grew directly out of Havoc's efforts.

Currently, Jon Trowbridge is Guppi's primary developer. Development is taking place in Chicago, IL, USA, in various locations around Hyde Park, the neighborhood that is the home of the University of Chicago. All Guppi bugs and misfeatures are Jon's fault.

Plenty of other people have contributed to Guppi. They are listed in Guppi's AUTHORS file, a semi-recent copy of which is attached below.
