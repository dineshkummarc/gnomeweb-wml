9.3. Date and Time formatting

Gnumeric supports a wide variety of date and time formatting.

These formats can be used to convert an existing time serial number into a new more readable format, or dates can be entered in these formats and Gnumeric will automatically convert them to a serial number with the proper cell format. See Section 6.1.1 for more information.

In Example 9-3 'd' is a day, 'm' is a month, 'y' is a year, 'h' is an hour, and 's' is a second.

Example 9-3. Date and Time Formatting.
