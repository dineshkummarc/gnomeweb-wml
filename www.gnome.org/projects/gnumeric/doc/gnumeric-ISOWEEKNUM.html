ISOWEEKNUM

Name

ISOWEEKNUM —

Synopsis

ISOWEEKNUM (date)

Description

ISOWEEKNUM returns the ISO 8601 week number of @date.

An ISO 8601 week starts on Monday. Weeks are numbered from 1. A week including days from two different years is assigned to the year which includes the most days. This means that Dec 31 could be in week 1 of the following year, and Jan 1 could be in week 52 or 53 of the previous year. ISOWEEKNUM returns the week number, while ISOYEAR returns the year the week is assigned to.

* ISOWEEKNUM returns #NUM! if date is invalid.

Examples

If A1 contains 12/21/00 then ISOWEEKNUM(A1)=51

See also

WEEKNUM, ISOYEAR.
