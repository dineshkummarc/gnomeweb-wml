8.1. Printing to a Printer or a File.

The Print dialog includes some options related to printing. More detailed options related to printing are in the Page Setup dialog, detailed in Page Setup. Press the Print to print out the workbook. Press the Preview button to display the Print Preview dialog.

Figure 8.1. Print Dialog

Click on the Printer radiobutton and click on Print to send the document to your printer. You can also send the printed document to a postscript file.

You may also select a multiple number of copies, or print a subset of the pages of the document.

If you are not sure which pages you need, click on the preview button to see the layout of the printed document.

If you need to change your page size or scale the output to fit properly onto your page, see the Page Setup.
