G_PRODUCT

Name

G_PRODUCT —

Synopsis

G_PRODUCT(value1, value2, ...)

Description

G_PRODUCT returns the product of all the values and cells referenced in the argument list.

* Empty cells are ignored and the empty product is 1.

Examples

G_PRODUCT(2,5,9) equals 90.

See also

SUM, COUNT.
