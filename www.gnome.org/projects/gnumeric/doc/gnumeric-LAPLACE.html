LAPLACE

Name

LAPLACE —

Synopsis

LAPLACE(x,a)

Description

LAPLACE returns the probability density p(x) at @x for Laplace distribution with mean @a.

Examples

LAPLACE(0.4,1).

See also

RANDLAPLACE.
