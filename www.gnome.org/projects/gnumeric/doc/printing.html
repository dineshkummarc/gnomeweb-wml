Chapter 11. Printing

11.1. Printing to a Printer or a File.

The Print dialog includes some options related to printing. More detailed options related to printing are in the Print Setup dialog, detailed in Section 11.2 Press the Print to print out the workbook. Press the Preview button to display the Print Preview dialog.

Figure 11-1. Print Dialog

Click on the Printer radiobutton and click on Print to send the document to your printer. You can also send the printed document to a postscript file.

You may also select a multiple number of copies, or print a subset of the pages of the document.

If you are not sure which pages you need, click on the preview button to see the layout of the printed document.

If you need to change your page size or scale the output to fit properly onto your page, see the Section 11.2
