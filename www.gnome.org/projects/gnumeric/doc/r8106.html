EURO

Name

EURO -- 

Synopsis

EURO(currency)

Description

EURO converts one Euro to a given national currency in the European monetary union. @currency is one of the following:

ATS (Austria)

BEF (Belgium)

DEM (Germany)

ESP (Spain)

FIM (Finland)

FRF (France)

IEP (Ireland)

ITL (Italy)

LUF (Luxemburg)

NLG (Netherlands)

PTE (Portugal)

If the given @currency is other than one of the above, EURO returns #NUM! error.

Examples

EURO("DEM") returns 1.95583.
