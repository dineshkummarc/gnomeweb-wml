Synopsis

VARP(b1, b2, ...)

Description

VARP calculates the variance of an entire population.

VARP is also known as the N-variance.

Examples

Let us assume that the cells A1, A2, ..., A5 contain numbers 11.4, 17.3, 21.3, 25.9, and 40.1. Then

VARP(A1:A5) equals 94.112.
