Accuracy of Calculations

Floating-Point Issues

Gnumeric uses finite precision in all its calculations. More precisely, Gnumeric uses your computer's native floating-point values which typically means 53 binary digits worth of precision. That is roughly the same as about 14 decimal digits worth of precision.

  • Don't expect 21.1-20.9 to be precisely equal to 0.2. This comes as a bad surprise to most people and is a consequence of a binary floating point system being used. In a binary system there no exact representation of 21.1, 20.9, and 0.2.
