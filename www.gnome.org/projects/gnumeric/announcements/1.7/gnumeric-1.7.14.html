Gnumeric 1.7.14 aka "TBD" is now available.

This is the second beta release in the quest for 1.8.

A large number of crashes were identified, primarily by Sum1, and promptly fixed. Also, a few of the feature regressions for printing were fixed; one or two might still be present. Finally, ODF import saw improvements, mainly in charts.

This release requires the concurrently released Goffice 0.5.2, see below.
