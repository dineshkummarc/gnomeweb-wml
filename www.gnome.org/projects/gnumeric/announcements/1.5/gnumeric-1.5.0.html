Gnumeric 1.5.0 aka "RTL calm before the storm" is now available.

RTL layout and import/export has been added along with splined lines in plots. There are also a small collection of patches and stability improvements. This release is actually quite stable, and is intended to serve as a snapshot before the goffice split patch lands in a couple of hours.
