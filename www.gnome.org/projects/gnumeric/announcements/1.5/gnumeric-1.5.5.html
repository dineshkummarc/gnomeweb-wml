Gnumeric 1.5.5 aka "Problem Solved" is now available.

This is a development version of Gnumeric. The main news is that the solver should be working again.

(Gnumeric 1.5.4 was never announced to a wider audience as it had a serious problem affecting the Excel plugin.)
