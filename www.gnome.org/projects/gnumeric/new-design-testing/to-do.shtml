Things we plan to do in Gnumeric

These are our immediate (and not-so-immediate) plans for new features or just work to be done in Gnumeric. Feel free to contact us if you want to help or just discuss any of these.

The developers' scratch pad is usually up to date, but may not spell things out in details.

Immediate plans

  1. Stabilize, stabilize, stabilize!

Intermediate plans

  1. Format engine rewrite. Our current number formatting and matching engines are based directly on Excel format strings. That is truly sub-optimal and needs to be rewritten in terms of sensible objects.
  2. Function documentation. Currently the documentation for functions like SUM, VAR, and so on is one large string per function. That makes life hard on translators and invites large amounts of duplication.

Wish-list (long-range plans)

  1. Pivot tables. This is probably just a matter of time and effort.
