Does Gnumeric support more than 256 columns?

Yes, but it requires a re-compile. Edit SHEET_MAX_COLS and SHEET_MAX_ROWS in src/gnumeric.h and re-compile. Note, that performance might be an issue if you make either of these huge.

Does Gnumeric support pivot tables?

Not yet. There are no fundamental problems; it's just a matter of man power and priorities.

Does Gnumeric support rich text?

Yes.

Does Gnumeric support rotated text?

Yes, although this has not yet been released. Expect to see it in version 1.4.2.
