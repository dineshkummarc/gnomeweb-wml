Downloading

There are two ways of downloading GST archives:

  • Through ftp.gnome.org, you can get the latest tarball.
  • Through CVS you can get the latest development code, all you have to do is write:

     $export CVSROOT=':pserver:anonymous@anoncvs.gnome.org:/cvs/gnome'
     $cvs login
      [when prompted for password press enter]
     $cvs -z3 co gnome-system-tools

NOTE: you must know that these tools are still under development, and, as they treat with basic configuration files, they might break your system configuration.
