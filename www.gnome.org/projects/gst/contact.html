Contacting us

If you'd like to join to our project, of if only you want to expose an opinion, feel free to contact us by:

  • IRC: you can join us in the #gst channel on irc.gnome.org to discuse the project in detail.
  • Mailing List: you can subscribe to our GST Mailing list at Ximian. You can also view the historic archives here.
