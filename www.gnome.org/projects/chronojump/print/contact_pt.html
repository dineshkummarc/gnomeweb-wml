Instructions
If you want to contact with project developers or with other users, you need to subscribe to one of the mailing lists. Just follow this procedure:
  1. Click on one of the subscribe links.
  2. Write your e-mail address (check that it's correct), and your name (optional).
  3. Write a non valuable password, something like "1234" where it's written password.
  4. Click on Subscribe.
  5. An e-mail will be send to you..
  6. Reply the e-mail without modify anything. Just click at reply.
  7. Another e-mail will be sent to you.
Now you are subscribed and you can write to the list sending an e-mail to:
  • chronojump-list@gnome.org (for chronojump-list)
  • chronojump-devel-list@gnome.org (for chronojump-devel-list)
Your e-mail will arive to all the members of the list.

When you are subscribed, you can send e-mail introducing yourself.
