Introduction

Printable version
ChronoJump is a complete multiplatform system for measurement, management and statistics of the jump events.
Chronojump uses a contact platform suitable for two events (in-platform, out-platform), and also a chronometer printed circuit designed ad-hoc in order to obtain precise and trustworthy measurements.

The flight time of a jump is an indicator of the elevation of the Center of gravity of the jumper (as long as the starting and arrival position are the same).


Scheme of management software (ChronoJump) + chronometer (Chronopic) + platform of contacts.

ChronoJump can also be used for timing races even with intervals and rhythms.

License

ChronoJump is free software  distributed under the GPL license. The program and chronometer source code is available, it is also possible to modify and redistribute.

The chronometer and the contacts platform come along with construction guidelines.

It is not possible to verify the validity and fiability of a digital measuring instrument if the source code is not available. Therefore Chronojump is appopriate to investigations.
