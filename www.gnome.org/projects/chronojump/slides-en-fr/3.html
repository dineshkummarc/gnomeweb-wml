Chronojump - jump Jump is a basic motor skill present in most physical activities. How to measure jump hability with accuracy and objectiveness has been studied by different kinesiologists*. Jump measurement is a way to assess low extremities strength and power. It has been described a method to estimate the percentage of fast muscle fibers without a biopsy*. Each sport has it's own physical requeriments, thus when there's a need to evaluate jump in a targeted sport, a specific jump should be used*.
