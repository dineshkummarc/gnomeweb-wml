Helping with Evince

Triage Bugzilla

Use this link for looking at open bugs with Evince. Please make sure the new and unconfirmed bugs conform the the bug reporting guidelines laid out in the testing section.

The GNOME Bugsquad has a good triage guide for people new to bugzilla and GNOME. Read up on that before doing any triage work with bugzilla and if you know that please also read our bug reporting guidelines in the testing section.

Distribution Support

Evince needs as much testing and widespread distribution as possible in order to satisfy its goal of becoming the default document viewer in GNOME and replace the craziness that exists.

Ask your favorite distribution to package Evince and use it at your default document viewer.

Bugzilla

The Evince bugzilla is found on the GNOME servers.

Reporting a Bug

Please follow the Proper reporting guidelines and fill out the form here to report a problem with Evince.


Distributions

A big way to help out the Evince project is to get a package built for your distribution. See your distribution guidelines for building a package and email the Evince mailing list to let us know. We'll update this page with distributions as we know about them packaging evince.
