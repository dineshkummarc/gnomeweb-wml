Characteristics of the AT-SPI :

  • no offscreen model needed
  • AT talks only to AT-SPI services
  • requires bridges and implementations for application UI toolkits
    • non-compliant apps can't play
    • lowers barriers to compliance
  • uniform API for heterogeneous environment
    • scripting not requred, but may enhance
    • cognitive impairment opportunities
  • fully network-transparent
