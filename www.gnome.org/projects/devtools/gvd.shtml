GNU Visual Debugger

GVD, the GNU Visual Debugger, is an extensible graphical debugger licensed under the GNU General Public License and written in Ada using GNAT and the GtkAda graphical toolkit.

Why we chose GtkAda instead of Gtk+

For several reasons. One of them was to push GtkAda to its limits and verify that this toolkit was suitable for complex GUI development. Another reason was to reduce the time we spent developing GVD. Using GtkAda and GNAT, we can decrease time to market by a factor of two to four over any other language/compiler.
