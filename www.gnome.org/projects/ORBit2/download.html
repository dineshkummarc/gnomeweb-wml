Download

ORBit2 depends on two further projects glib2 and linc, the latter is used for low level connection management. If you don't want to compile ORBit2 applications, developer packages can be ignored.

SuSE-8.1 (i386)

glib2 2.0.7 (devel)
linc 0.5.2 (devel)
orbit2 2.4.1 (devel)

Redhat-9.0 (i386)

glib2-2.2.1-1.i386.rpm (devel)
linc-1.0.1-1.i386.rpm (devel)
ORBit2-2.6.0-2.i386.rpm (devel)

Install from CVS

GNOME CVS explains how to check out newest releases from CVS repository, being used for concurrent development in teams. You need to check out and compile these three modules in given order: glib2, linc and ORBit2.
