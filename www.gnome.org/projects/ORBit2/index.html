ORBit2

ORBit2 is a CORBA 2.2-compliant Object Request Broker (ORB) featuring mature C and Perl bindings. Bindings (in various degrees of completeness) are also available for C++, Lisp, Pascal, Python, Ruby, and TCL; others are in-progress. It supports POA, DII, DSI, TypeCode, Any, IR and IIOP. Optional features including INS and threading are available. ORBit2 is engineered for the desktop workstation environment, with a focus on performance, low resource usage, and security. The core ORB is written in C, and runs under Linux, UNIX (BSD, Solaris, HP-UX, ...), and Windows. ORBit2 is developed and released as open source software under GPL/LGPL.
